Chhrapati shivaji Maharaj

About: Shivaji Shahaji Bhosale, Marathi pronunciation: 19 February 1630 to 3 April 1680, was an Indian ruler and a member of the Bhonsle dynasty. Shivaji carved out his own independent kingdom from the declining Adilshahi Sultanate of Bijapur that formed the genesis of the Maratha Confederacy. In 1674, he was formally crowned the Chhatrapati of his realm at Raigad Fort.
